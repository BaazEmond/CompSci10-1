How to make Kraft Dinner

Learn how to make a simple Kraft Dinner Mac n Cheese .

Ingredients

  • 1 box of Kraft Dinner maccaroni and cheese
  • 1/3 of a cup of milk
  • 1 tablespoon of butter

Instructions

For Microwave:

  1. Empty pasta into a 6 cup microwaveable bowl.
  2. Add 1-3/4 cups of HOT water and stir.
  3. Microwave the bowl on high for 10 to 11 minutes, or until the water is absorbed. Stir every 3 minutes.
  4. Take the Kraft Dinner out of the microwave, and add in your milk and butter.
  5. Mix Well, and Enjoy!

For Stove Top:

  1. Boil 6 cups of water.
  2. Add your pasta to the mix.
  3. Let the pasta cook for 7 to 9 minutes.
  4. Stir the mixture occasionally.
  5. When the mixture is done cooking, drain the mixture.
  6. Return the drained mixture to the original pan.
  7. Add in your butter, cheese powder and milk.
  8. Stir Well, and Enjoy!
